Peter Zhang advises clients on a variety of corporate and securities matters with a focus on capital-raising and financial regulatory compliance, and has extensive experience with business and investment-based immigration.

Peter represents established as well as emerging companies at all stages of the business cycle, and he has in-depth experience advising overseas entrepreneurs and high-net-worth investors on accessing the U.S. markets. By applying an uncompromising focus to his clients’ businesses and objectives, Peter consistently delivers creative and pragmatic solutions to the complex and often novel issues they face.

Peter has counseled clients across a broad range of industry sectors—including financial services, real estate, blockchain technology and healthcare—on matters involving corporate governance, regulatory compliance, and private securities offerings. He has extensive experience working with new capital market initiatives and has guided many clients with sophisticated needs through complex and dynamic regulatory environments. Peter has particular expertise with Regulation Crowdfunding (Reg CF), Rule D 506(c), Regulation A+, and the EB-5 Immigrant Investor Program, as well as with obtaining regulatory approvals from the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) and the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority (FINRA).

One of Peter’s select areas of practice has been investment-based immigration. He regularly represents investors and entrepreneurs from diverse national and cultural backgrounds, as well as regional centers, developers, business owners, and other stakeholders.

Reflective of the firm’s core values, Peter prioritizes the cultivation of long-term client relationships by consistently delivering world-class legal representation. Peter was recognized by Super Lawyers as a “Rising Star” (age 40 or under) in the area of securities and corporate finance.

Peter is fluent in English and Mandarin Chinese.
